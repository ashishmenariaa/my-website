Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

At SD Zone, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our trading indicators and services.

1 Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register for SD Zone services, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Login Credentials: Username and encrypted password
  • Profile Data: Trading preferences and settings you configure

Payment Information

When you subscribe to our services:

  • Payment transactions are processed securely through Razorpay
  • We do NOT store your credit card, debit card, or banking details
  • We only store transaction IDs and payment status for record-keeping
  • Razorpay handles all sensitive payment data according to PCI-DSS standards

Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our services:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Metrics: How you interact with our indicators and platform
  • Log Data: Access times, pages viewed, actions performed
  • Technical Data: Error reports and performance metrics
Note: We do NOT collect or store your trading account credentials, brokerage information, or actual trading positions. We only provide indicators for your use on TradingView.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

Service Delivery

  • Create and manage your SD Zone account
  • Provide access to our trading indicators and tools
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Deliver indicator updates and improvements

Communication

  • Send account confirmation emails after registration
  • Notify you about subscription expiry and renewal reminders
  • Send password reset emails when requested
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important service updates and announcements

Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our indicators
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user needs
  • Enhance overall user experience

Security and Compliance

  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized access
  • Enforce our Terms and Conditions
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
We Will Never: Sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3 How We Store Your Data

Data Storage Infrastructure

  • Database: Your data is stored securely in MongoDB databases
  • Hosting: Our services are hosted on Render cloud infrastructure
  • Location: Data is primarily stored on servers located in secure data centers
  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit (using SSL/TLS) and at rest

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data:

  • Encrypted password storage using secure hashing algorithms
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure API communications with authentication tokens
  • Access controls limiting who can view or modify data
  • Regular backups to prevent data loss
  • Monitoring systems to detect suspicious activity

Data Retention

  • We retain your account data while your subscription is active
  • After subscription expiry, data is retained for 90 days in case you renew
  • Transaction records are kept for 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • You can request data deletion by contacting our support team
Important: While we implement robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously work to protect your data.

4 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may access your data:

Razorpay (Payment Processing)

  • Handles all payment transactions securely
  • Processes and stores your payment information
  • Subject to Razorpay's Privacy Policy
  • PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway

TradingView (Indicator Platform)

  • Our indicators run on the TradingView platform
  • TradingView may collect data about your chart usage
  • Subject to TradingView's Privacy Policy
  • We do not control TradingView's data practices

Render (Cloud Hosting)

  • Hosts our application and database infrastructure
  • Provides server and network security
  • Subject to Render's security and privacy standards

Email Service Provider

  • Used to send transactional emails (confirmations, password resets, reminders)
  • Has access to your email address for delivery purposes
  • Does not use your email for marketing without consent
Third-Party Policies: We carefully select service providers with strong privacy and security standards. However, we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their respective privacy policies.

5 Cookies and Tracking

Current Cookie Usage

At launch, SD Zone uses minimal cookies primarily for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for login authentication and session management
  • Security Cookies: Help protect against unauthorized access
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

Future Analytics

We may implement analytics tools in the future to improve our services:

  • If we add Google Analytics or similar tools, we will update this policy
  • You will be notified of any changes to our tracking practices
  • Analytics data will be anonymized where possible
  • You will have the option to opt-out of non-essential tracking

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones
  • Blocking essential cookies may affect site functionality
  • Refer to your browser's help documentation for specific instructions

6 Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access and Portability

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • We will provide this information within 30 days of your request

Correction and Updates

  • Right to Rectification: Correct any inaccurate personal information
  • Update your account details directly through your profile settings
  • Contact support for assistance with data corrections

Deletion

  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Contact help.sdzone@gmail.com to request account deletion
  • We will delete data within 30 days, except where legally required to retain it
  • Some data may be retained for legal, accounting, or security purposes

Objection and Restriction

  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data for certain purposes
  • Right to Restriction: Request limitation of how we process your data

Withdrawal of Consent

  • You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • This does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal
  • May impact our ability to provide certain services
Exercise Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at help.sdzone@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.
