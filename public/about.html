About SD Zone

Empowering traders with precision supply & demand zone analysis since 2020

🎯 Our Mission

To democratize professional-grade trading education and signals, making institutional-level analysis accessible to retail traders across India.

We believe every trader deserves access to high-quality market insights without the complexity and cost typically associated with professional trading systems.

🚀 Our Vision

To become India's most trusted supply and demand zone trading platform, known for accuracy, transparency, and genuine trader education.

We envision a community where traders make informed decisions based on solid technical analysis rather than emotions or speculation.

📖 Our Story

SD Zone was born from a simple observation: most retail traders struggle not because they lack intelligence or dedication, but because they don't have access to the right tools and education. The institutional knowledge of supply and demand zones—where big money enters and exits the market—was kept behind expensive paywalls or complex training programs.

In 2020, we started sharing free educational content about supply and demand trading on social media. The response was overwhelming. Thousands of traders resonated with our straightforward, no-nonsense approach to technical analysis. This community grew to over 11,000 followers who trusted our insights.

Recognizing the need for a more comprehensive solution, we launched SD Zone—a platform that combines precision trading signals with educational content, all delivered through an intuitive interface that works seamlessly with TradingView.

Today, SD Zone serves thousands of active traders, maintaining an 87% accuracy rate while staying true to our founding principle: education first, profits second.
